About This Site

Made by fans, for fans

Nayantharafanmade is an independent, fan-run tribute project celebrating the work and journey of actress Nayanthara, known to fans as the "Lady Superstar." The site brings together a gallery, curated videos, a filmography timeline, an awards archive and a fan zone of free downloads.

We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by Nayanthara or her official representatives. This is a fan-made passion project built with respect and admiration for her work.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this the official website of Nayanthara?

No. This is an unofficial, fan-made tribute site and is not affiliated with Nayanthara or her representatives.

What can I find here?

A photo gallery, curated videos, a filmography timeline, an awards timeline, and a fan zone with wallpapers, quotes and fan edits.

Who runs this site?

An independent fan community that also maintains a dedicated YouTube channel.

Can I use the wallpapers commercially?

Downloads are provided for personal, non-commercial use only.

Does this site show real-time video uploads?

The Videos section is built to pull the latest uploads from @nayantharafanmadeofficial via the YouTube Data API, with curated cards shown when live data isn't configured.

Can I filter movies by language?

Yes, the Movies timeline includes filter buttons for Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam and Hindi releases.

Is the site installable as an app?

Yes, Nayantharafanmade is a Progressive Web App with basic offline support and can be installed on Android, iPhone and desktop.
